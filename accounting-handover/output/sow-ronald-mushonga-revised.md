## 1. Scope of Engagement

The Consultant shall provide fractional CFO services to Saint Raphael (Pty) Ltd and its associated entities, ensuring continuity of financial operations following the departure of the incumbent Financial Manager on 23 February 2026.

The role encompasses financial oversight, bookkeeping, reporting, compliance tracking, invoicing quality control, and facilitating the transition of accounting service providers across the group.

The associated entities in scope are:
- Saint Raphael (Pty) Ltd
- Dr James La Porta Inc
- La Porta Industries (Pty) Ltd
- Sanctura Limited (UK) - liaison with Kinari / Capricorn Capital Services
- Sanctura Holdings (UK) - liaison with Kinari / Capricorn Capital Services
