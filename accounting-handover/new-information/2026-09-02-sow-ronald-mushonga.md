The Consultant shall provide fractional CFO services to Saint Raphael (Pty) Ltd and its associated entities, ensuring continuity of financial operations following the departure of the incumbent Financial Manager on 23 February 2026.

The associated entities in scope are:

- Sanctura Limited (UK) - liaison with Kinari
