#HBOT Specifications

## The un

* **Manufacturer:** Sechrist
* **Model:** 2500B
* **Atmospheric range:** **1.0 – 3.0 ATA**
* **Original manufacture year:** **1983**
* **Hull/Tube year:** **2007**

## Core performance / operating specs (commonly listed for the Sechrist 2500 series)

* **Maximum operating pressure:** **30 psi (≈ 3 ATA)** ([hcinc][1])
* **Pressure change rate (compression/decompression):** **1–5 psi/min (linear)** ([hcinc][1])
* **Emergency vent rate:** **1 psi/sec (linear)** ([hcinc][1])
* **Supply pressure (gas supply to system):** **50–70 psi** ([hcinc][1])
* **Purge/ventilation rate:** **240–400 LPM, adjustable** ([hcinc][1])

## Physical / space specs (Sechrist 2500 series listing)

* **Internal diameter:** **25 in** ([hcinc][1])
* **Internal length:** **86 in** ([hcinc][1])
* **External length:** **95 in** ([hcinc][1])
* **External width:** **36 in** ([hcinc][1])
* **External height:** **50 in** ([hcinc][1])
* **Weight:** **~1,140 lb** ([hcinc][1])

## System description (what the 2500B “is”)

A commonly referenced description of the **Model 2500B Monoplace Hyperbaric System** is that it’s designed to administer **100% oxygen and/or medical breathing air up to 3 ATA**, and that the system consists of a **double-hulled pressure chamber with a quick-opening door**, a **pneumatic pressure control system**, and an **intercommunication system**. ([Veterinary Hyperbaric Safety][2])

## Useful notes for your records (practical)

* **3.0 ATA ≈ 30 psig** (that mapping is how many spec sheets present the max pressure) ([hcinc][1])
* Specs can vary slightly by **configuration, retrofit history, and control panel version**, especially with an older base unit + newer hull/tube.
