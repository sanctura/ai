# Directory Analysis: Marketing Engagement with Shift One Digital

## Directory Overview

This directory contains documents related to the engagement of a digital marketing agency, Shift One Digital, by Sanctura, a healthcare organization. The primary purpose is to manage the contractual, financial, and strategic aspects of a 90-day pilot marketing program scheduled to start in January 2026.

## Key Files

*   **`Files/Sanctura x S1 SLA.pdf`**: The master Service Level Agreement provided by Shift One Digital. It outlines the legal terms, duration, fees, and intellectual property rights for the engagement.

*   **`sla-feedback.md`**: A draft email addressed to Shift One Digital. It contains a detailed list of requested amendments to the SLA to better align the contract with a 90-day pilot structure, reduce commercial risk, and establish fairer terms for Sanctura.

*   **`Files/initial acceptance correspondance.txt`**: Key email correspondence from Sanctura to Shift One, confirming board approval for a "90-day Phase 1 engagement." This document is crucial as it establishes the original intent for a flexible pilot, not a long-term contract.

*   **`Files/ShiftOne - Proposal.pdf`**: The original proposal from Shift One Digital. It details their recommended services, approach, and the rationale for their engagement model.

*   **`Files/Sanctura Draft Digital Marketing Budget 2026 _ S1 - ZAR.xlsx`**: A spreadsheet outlining the proposed digital marketing budget for the activities to be managed by Shift One.

## Usage

The contents of this directory are used to negotiate, finalize, and manage the commercial and legal relationship with the marketing partner, Shift One Digital. It serves as a central repository for the key documents defining the scope, objectives, and terms of the marketing partnership, with a strong focus on ensuring the initial 90-day pilot phase is structured correctly before committing to a larger-scale engagement.
