### Key Insight: The Inc Loss is Structural
Inc shows a R9M loss YTD but this is by design - R11.4M of its expenses are management fees paid to Saint Raphael (SANC - Management). Inc is the revenue collector that passes costs through to the operating entity. The **combined SA position** (Inc + Saint Raphael) shows the true picture:
- Combined SA Revenue (eliminating intercompany): ~R16.1M
- Combined SA Net Position: R9.03M loss + R2.89M profit = **R6.13M combined loss**
- This loss is driven by high operating costs, particularly travel (R1.9M combined), depreciation (R2.7M), consulting fees, and the fact that the entity structure creates cost duplication.
