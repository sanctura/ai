## Key Observations

1. **Rapid direct sales growth:** From £12K (Mar) to £88K (Nov) - 7x growth in 9 months. Strong validation of the UK clinic model.
2. **Agency fees nearly eliminated:** Were £48K/month (Jun), now virtually zero. Margin improvement as agency COS was very high.
3. **mEHT is the star service:** 47% of itemised revenue. High-value treatments (£300-450 per session, 81 sessions in Nov).
4. **Infusions are high volume:** 70 infusions at £125 = strong recurring revenue stream.
5. **Sanctura Holdings loan (£985K):** The entity is heavily funded by its parent. This is the startup investment.
6. **Rent at £23,750/month (£285K/year):** Significant fixed cost for the London location. Deposit of £204K held.
7. **Leasehold improvements (£421K):** Major investment in the clinic fitout.
8. **Accrued rent (£99K):** Rent in arrears - needs attention.
9. **Approaching breakeven:** Nov was a slight loss (-£14K) but Oct was profitable (+£18K). With growing direct sales and falling agency fees, monthly profitability is within reach.
10. **Utilities high (£13K/month):** Likely reflects the medical equipment power requirements.
11. **Pathology is new (from Oct 2025):** New revenue stream - £5.2K YTD. Worth monitoring.
