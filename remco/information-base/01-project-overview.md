# 2026 Staff Increase Review - Project Overview

## Key Dates
| Milestone | Date |
|-----------|------|
| Last SA salary increase | June 2024 |
| Interim bonus paid | June 2025 |
| UK operations commenced (Newman Street) | May 2025 |
| Proposed increase effective date | March 2026 (start of financial year) |
| Review deadline | End of February 2026 |

## Scope
- **Total staff requiring review**: ~20 eligible staff
- **Locations**: Cape Town (~14), London (~5), Global (~8)
- **Out of scope**: Founders Associate position (off the cards), Locums, Consultants

## Proposed Increase Ranges
| Region | Proposed Range | Economic Justification |
|--------|---------------|------------------------|
| South Africa | 7-9% | CPI 3.2-3.6%, electricity +9-12%, lending rate 10.25% |
| United Kingdom | 4-5% | CPIH 3.6%, electricity +2%, lending rate 3.75% |

## Eligibility Rule
- **< 6 months service or on probation**: 0% increase
- **> 6 months service**: Eligible for standard increase range

## Key Principles (from December 2025 framework discussion)
1. Increases are **not contractually guaranteed** - at company's sole discretion
2. Must align with company performance and financial sustainability
3. Increases aligned with **financial year (March)**, not calendar dates
4. Review conducted separately for SA and UK economic conditions
