# Increase Framework & Principles

## Agreed Framework (December 2025 Remuneration Committee)

### 1. Eligibility Criteria

**Staff who qualify must meet ALL of the following:**
- Employed for more than 6 months (or 1 year - to be confirmed)
- Not currently on probation
- Not newly appointed
- Not under performance or role-related review

**Staff who do NOT qualify:**
- Anyone employed less than 6 months (or 1 year)
- Anyone still in probation
- Anyone under discussion for role, performance, or structural reasons
- Anyone who has already received a promotion or salary increase since the last review

### 2. Contractual Position

- Salary increases are **not contractually guaranteed**
- Not written into employment contracts
- Remain at **sole discretion of the company**
- Must align with:
  - Company performance
  - Financial sustainability
  - Strategic priorities

### 3. Financial Year Alignment

- Increases aligned with **new financial year (March)** rather than calendar dates
- Final approval linked to:
  - Company performance
  - Financial outcomes for prior year
  - Forward-looking affordability

### 4. Regional Approach

- Review conducted **simultaneously** for both regions
- Consistency in approach
- Allow for **regional economic differences** in % ranges

### 5. Financial Impact Assessment

A detailed schedule to be prepared showing:
- Staff name
- Start date
- Current salary
- Eligible increase range

Used to:
- Quantify total cost impact
- Assess affordability
- Support final committee decision
