# Project Overview: Patient Conversion Engine

## Objective
Standardize lead management and clinical conversion across ShiftOne Digital's UK and South African clinics using GoHighLevel (GHL) CRM.

## Scope
- Single GHL license serving two clinic locations (UK and SA)
- Two core workflows covering the full patient journey from inquiry to treatment
- Manual communication only (no automated patient-facing messages at this stage)
