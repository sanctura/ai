# Strategic Positioning

## Competitive Landscape

### Current UK Adoption
Hilotherapy is being adopted across major UK oncology providers:
- **GenesisCare UK** - Active user, published UKONS poster (Anna McNally)
- **The Royal Marsden NHS Foundation Trust** - Service evaluation completed (Laura Collantes-Espinosa), published UKONS poster August 2025
- **Ramsay Health Care UK** - Early adoption
- **Spire Healthcare** - Early adoption
- **Selected NHS centres** - Supported through charitable funding

### Sanctura vs Competitors

**vs Lloyd's (current competitor)**:
- Lloyd's offers home-based Paxman scalp cooling systems - bulky, logistically complex
- Sanctura would offer **in-clinic, supervised, in-chair supportive cooling** within the infusion pathway
- One-stop service: chemotherapy + cooling in same visit = seamless patient experience
- Medical-grade CIPN prevention (hands/feet) - Lloyd's does not offer this

**vs Frozen Gloves (traditional approach)**:
- Hilotherapy is clinically superior (p=0.013 for Grade 2+ side-effects)
- Significantly more comfortable for patients (p<0.0001)
- More practical for staff (no glove changes every 45 min)
- Consumer products like SuziePads lack reproducible temperature regulation and governance oversight

## Strategic Benefits

### 1. Patient Attraction & Retention
- Patients actively choose where to receive chemotherapy based on available supportive care
- Scalp cooling in particular influences treatment location decisions
- Prof. Stebbing has confirmed he would **refer patients and recommend this treatment**
- Creates a differentiated, premium patient experience

### 2. Integrated Oncology Positioning
- Places Sanctura among the **few UK centres offering fully integrated chemotherapy delivery** with:
  - Medical-grade CIPN prevention
  - Scalp cooling
  - In the same clinical setting
- Forward-thinking precision oncology provider image

### 3. Research & Academic Credentials
- Opportunity to generate **publishable outcome data** in collaboration with Prof. Stebbing
- Tracks clinical impact using CTCAE neuropathy grading and patient-reported outcomes
- Adds to Sanctura's academic profile and credibility

### 4. Low-Risk Entry
- 6-month lease = minimal capital commitment
- Service self-funds under conservative assumptions
- Option to walk away after 6 months if uptake disappoints
- If successful, lease payments credit toward purchase - no sunk cost
