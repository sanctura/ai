# Implementation Plan

## Phase 1: 6-Month Lease Pilot (Feb 2026 - Aug 2026)

### Setup
- Board approval for £3,960 (inc VAT) lease payment
- Invoice HL3076 dated 11/02/2026 - due on receipt
- Delivery to: Sanctura, 24 Newman Street, Fitzrovia, London, W1T 1PJ
- Lease period: 16/02/2026 to 15/08/2026

### Equipment
- 1x Hilotherm CIPN device & trolley
- 1x Hilotherm CIA (scalp cooling) device & trolley

### Service Model
- Optional self-funded patient add-on to chemotherapy treatment
- Patient fees: £75-£195 per infusion session depending on service
- In-chair, supervised cooling within existing infusion pathway
- Nurse-led delivery (Maria Vega as clinical lead)

### Clinical Monitoring
- **CTCAE neuropathy grading** at each cycle
- **Patient-reported outcomes** (PRO-CTCAE)
- Track: neuropathy incidence/severity, treatment modifications, patient satisfaction
- Financial performance monitoring

### Success Metrics
- Patient uptake (target: 6-8 patients/month)
- Revenue vs lease cost (target: self-sustaining)
- Clinical outcomes (CIPN grade reduction)
- Patient satisfaction scores
- Referral pipeline from Prof. Stebbing

## Phase 2: Review & Decision (Aug 2026)

### Evaluation Criteria
- Uptake and utilisation rates
- Clinical outcomes data
- Financial performance (revenue vs costs)
- Patient and clinician feedback
- Impact on chemotherapy patient volumes

### If Justified - Transition to Capital Purchase
- Lease payments (£3,300 ex VAT) credited toward purchase price
- Combined CIPN + CIA system: £23,300
- Net cost after credit: £20,000
- Stronger long-term business case with no ongoing lease payments

### If Not Justified
- Return equipment at end of lease
- Total exposure limited to £3,960
- Valuable data collected regardless
