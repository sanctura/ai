## Revenue Projections

### Conservative Scenario (6-8 patients/month across all options)

**Monthly revenue estimate** (using mid-range fees):
- Assume 4 CIPN patients @ £87.50 avg = £350/month
- Assume 2 scalp cooling patients @ £135 avg = £270/month
- Assume 2 integrated patients @ £185 avg = £370/month
- **Total monthly revenue: ~£990/month**
- **6-month revenue: ~£5,940**

**vs 6-month lease cost: £3,300 (ex VAT)**

**Net margin on pilot: ~£2,640 before consumables**

### Break-Even Analysis

At lowest fees:
- CIPN only @ £75: need ~44 sessions over 6 months to cover lease (7.3/month)
- Scalp cooling only @ £120: need ~28 sessions over 6 months (4.6/month)
- Combined @ £175: need ~19 sessions over 6 months (3.2/month)

**The lease cost is covered under conservative utilisation assumptions.**

## Path to Capital Purchase

If pilot succeeds:
- 6-month lease of £3,300 credited toward purchase
- Combined system purchase: £23,300 - £3,300 credit = **£20,000 remaining**
- At ~£990/month revenue, ROI on remaining capital in ~20 months
- Long-term: no ongoing lease costs, only consumables
