# Stock Categories

Items from the Xero inventory have been categorised into the following groups based on their codes, descriptions, and tax treatment.

## Category 1: IV Concentrates & Injectable Medications (CON10xx)

These are tracked, physical stock items used in infusion therapies and injections. Most are VAT exempt (classified under healthcare/medical supplies).

**Examples**: Ascorbic Acid, Curcumin IV, Glutathione, NAD+, Meyers Cocktail, Calcium Gluconate, Magnesium Sulphate, Ceftriaxone, Selen-Injekt

**Regulatory note**: Many of these are imported from Europe (German manufacturers like Pascoe, Kohler, Burghardt). IV medications are likely subject to MHRA import licensing.

**Tax treatment**: Exempt Income / Exempt Expenses

---

## Category 2: Oral Supplements (SUP1xxx)

Tracked, physical stock items sold directly to patients. These are oral vitamins, herbal remedies, and nutraceuticals.

**Examples**: Biosomo range, Curcumin capsules, Omega 3, Zinc, Resveratrol, DHEA, Modafinil, Reishi, Saffron

**Regulatory note**: Generally not classified as medications in the UK. Some items (e.g., Modafinil) may have specific regulatory requirements as they are prescription-only medicines.

**Tax treatment**: No VAT (zero-rated as health supplements)

---

## Category 3: Lab Tests & Pathology (Untracked)

Services rather than physical stock. These are blood tests, screening panels, and diagnostic services.

**Examples**: Full Blood Count, Liver Function, Thyroid (TSH, FT3, FT4), Tumour Markers (CA 125, CEA, PSA), Vitamin/Mineral levels, Sanctura Screening Panels, Signatera Tests

**Tax treatment**: Exempt Income (healthcare services)

**Note**: These don't require physical stock management but are important for billing and service tracking.

---

## Category 4: Consultations & Services (Untracked)

Professional services and facility fees.

**Examples**: Consultations (15min/30min/45min/1hr), Infusion Fee, Phlebotomy Fee, Facility Fee, HBOT Sessions, mEHT Hyperthermia, H2 Inhalation Therapy, Breathwork, Transcranial Photobiomodulation

**Tax treatment**: Mix of Exempt Income and No VAT
