# MO Stock Management - Kickoff Task Plan (Summary)

Two parallel workstreams running over 6 weeks.

---

## Workstream A: Stock Management Process

| Task | Description | Timing |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **A1** | **Document the current ("as-is") process** — map how ordering, receiving, storage, dispensing, and stock takes work today; capture pain points | Week 1-2 |
| **A2** | **Propose the new ("to-be") process** — design improved workflows, set reorder points, fill data gaps, write SOPs, recommend system/tooling | Week 2-4 |

### A1 covers:
- Ordering process | Receiving process | Storage setup | Dispensing/usage tracking | Stock take process | Pain points & gaps

### A2 covers:
- Target operating model | New ordering process | New receiving process | New dispensing process | New stock take process | Data gap cleanup (prices, suppliers, zero-stock items) | SOPs (×5) | System recommendation

---

## Workstream B: Regulatory & Legal Adherence

| Task | Description | Timing |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **B1** | **Classify all stock by regulatory status** — food supplement, licensed medicine, unlicensed special, controlled drug, POM | Week 1-2 |
| **B2** | **Import licensing review** — how German IV products are sourced, WDL/MIA licence requirements | Week 1-3 |
| **B3** | **Controlled drug compliance** — storage audit, register, Modafinil & Nebido requirements | Week 2-3 |
| **B4** | **Storage & handling audit** — temperature monitoring, segregation, cold chain | Week 2-4 |
| **B5** | **Designate a Responsible Person** — medicines management oversight role | Week 2-3 |
| **B6** | **Dispensing & record-keeping review** — batch traceability, MHRA record requirements | Week 3-5 |
| **B7** | **Engage regulatory advisor** — professional guidance on any grey areas | Week 3-6 |
