## Purpose
A detailed prompt to feed into an AI deep research tool (Gemini Deep Research, Perplexity, ChatGPT, etc.) to gather comprehensive information for planning the Merwitz family Vietnam trip.


## Notes for Using This Prompt

1. **Best tools to use:** Gemini Deep Research, Perplexity Pro, or ChatGPT with browsing enabled
2. **Consider splitting:** If the tool struggles with the length, you could split into "Segment A + Weather", "Segment B", and "Da Nang Activities" as separate queries
3. **Follow-up queries:** Once you have the initial research, you can drill deeper into specific destinations that interest you
