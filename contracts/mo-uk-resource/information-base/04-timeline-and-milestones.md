## Proposed Engagement Timeline

### Week 0: Contract & Setup (Pre-Start)
- Finalise and sign consulting agreement
- Set up access (email, systems, building access as needed)
- Confirm start date and initial schedule
- Brief relevant team members

### February: Month 1 (2 Days/Week)

**Focus: Orientation and Assessment**

#### Week 1-2: Onboarding
- Introduction to Sanctura leadership and key team members
- Orientation to current operations, systems, and challenges
- Review of existing documentation and processes
- Initial site/facility walkthrough

#### Week 3-4: Discovery
- Stakeholder interviews
- Observation of current workflows
- Identification of quick wins
- Draft initial findings and priorities

**Month 1 Milestone:** Written assessment of current state with prioritised recommendations

### March: Month 2 (3 Days/Week)

**Focus: Prioritisation and Initial Improvements**

- Deep dive into priority area(s) from Month 1 assessment
- Begin implementing quick wins
- Supplier review and engagement (if pharmacy/aseptic focus confirmed)
- Define operating rhythm proposals

**Month 2 Milestone:** First tangible improvements implemented; operating rhythm drafted

### April: Month 3+

**Focus: Embedding and Scaling**

- Review engagement effectiveness
- Decide on continuation/expansion
- Transition from discovery to ongoing operational support
- Evaluate longer-term/permanent role potential
