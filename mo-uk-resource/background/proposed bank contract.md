## **Bank Staff Agreement**

**This Agreement** is made on the date of the last signature below.

### **Between:**

**Sanctura Limited** (Company Number 09971213\) of Malta House, 36–38 Piccadilly, London, United Kingdom, W1J 0DP (“the Employer” or “we”),

and

**\[Name of Bank Staff Nurse\]** of **\[Address\]** (“the Worker” or “you”).

This Agreement sets out the terms and conditions under which you will be engaged to perform work on a bank or sessional basis for Sanctura Limited. It constitutes the written particulars required under Section 1 of the Employment Rights Act 1996\.

---

### **1\. Nature of Engagement**

1.1 Your engagement with us is on a *bank or sessional basis*. This means there is **no obligation** on us to offer you work, and no obligation on you to accept any work offered.

1.2 Each offer of work that you accept will form a separate assignment (an “Assignment”) under this Agreement. The terms of this Agreement will apply to each Assignment unless otherwise agreed in writing.

1.3 You acknowledge that this Agreement does not create any expectation of continuous employment or guaranteed hours. Periods between Assignments will not count towards continuity of employment, except as required by law.

---

### **2\. Job Title and Duties**

2.1 Your role is **Bank Staff Nurse**.

2.2 You will report to the **Practice Manager** or such other person as the Employer may designate from time to time.

2.3 Your duties are those normally associated with the position of Staff Nurse, as described in the job description provided to you, together with any reasonable additional duties that may be required to meet business needs.

2.4 You must hold and maintain all relevant current registration.

---

### **3\. Place of Work**

Your normal place of work is **24 Newman Street, London, W1T 1PJ**, although you may occasionally be required to work at other Sanctura Limited locations within reasonable travel distance.

---

### **4\. Right to Work**

You must have and maintain the right to work in the United Kingdom. You must notify us immediately if your immigration status changes. If you cease to have the right to work in the UK, this Agreement will terminate automatically without notice or compensation.

---

### **5\. Hours of Work**

5.1 Your working hours will vary according to the shifts offered and accepted.

5.2 The clinic’s operating hours are currently **8:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m.**, and shifts may include evenings or weekends as required.

5.3 Once a shift has been agreed, both parties will use reasonable efforts to honour that commitment. If it becomes necessary to cancel a shift, either party must give **reasonable notice**.

5.4 You are not entitled to payment for any period when you are not working or on an Assignment.

---

### **6\. Pay and Holiday**

6.1 You will be paid **£\[hourly rate\] per hour**.

6.2 You are required to submit a compliant invoice reflecting hours worked to the practice manager every month.

6.3 We may deduct from your pay any sums properly owed to us (for example, overpayments).

---

### **7\. Sickness**

7.1 If you are unable to attend a booked shift due to illness or injury, you must notify your line manager as soon as practicable, and in any event **no later than 30 minutes before** your scheduled start time.

7.2 You may be required to provide self-certification or medical certificates as required by law.

---

### **8\. Data Protection**

We will process personal data about you in accordance with our **Data Protection Policy** and **Privacy Notice**. This includes personal and sensitive information such as health data, required for operational and compliance purposes.

You must comply with all relevant data protection requirements during and after your engagement.

---

### **9\. Confidentiality**

9.1 During and after each Assignment, you must not disclose to any unauthorised person any confidential information belonging to Sanctura Limited, its patients, suppliers, or partners.

9.2 You must return all company property and delete any confidential information from personal devices upon request or at the end of any Assignment.

9.3 This obligation continues indefinitely after your engagement ends.

---

### **10\. Termination**

10.1 Either party may end this Agreement or any individual Assignment at any time by giving **reasonable notice**.

10.2 We may terminate your engagement immediately, without notice or payment, if you:

* commit gross misconduct;

* breach confidentiality or data protection obligations;

* lose your registration or right to work in the UK; or

* seriously breach any term of this Agreement.

10.3 There is no entitlement to pay for any period after an Assignment ends.

---

### **11\. Policies and Procedures**

You must comply with our policies and procedures, including clinical governance, safeguarding, infection control, data protection, and health & safety. These are available from your line manager or HR representative.

---

### **12\. Notices**

Any notice under this Agreement will be valid if:  
 (a) sent by first-class post to the recipient’s last known address;  
 (b) delivered personally; or  
 (c) sent by email to the last notified address.

Notices are deemed received 48 hours after posting or 24 hours after emailing.

---

### **13\. Governing Law**

This Agreement is governed by and interpreted according to the laws of **England and Wales**, and both parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts.

---

### **14\. Entire Agreement**

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between you and Sanctura Limited concerning bank work. It supersedes any previous verbal or written arrangements relating to your engagement.
