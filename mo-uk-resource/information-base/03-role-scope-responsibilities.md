# Role Scope and Responsibilities

## Overview

Mohammed's role spans several interconnected domains, leveraging his senior operational and pharmacy expertise rather than a narrow, task-based consultancy.

## Domain 1: Operational Efficiency & Clinic Operations

### Responsibilities
- Review and improve day-to-day operational efficiency
- Patient flow and patient management processes
- Management of existing patients and onboarding of new clients
- General operational systems and workflows across the practice

### Expected Outcomes
- Identified bottlenecks and inefficiencies
- Documented process improvements
- Streamlined patient journey

## Domain 2: Pharmacy & Aseptic / Regulatory Initiatives

### Responsibilities
- Laminar flow and cleanroom feasibility assessment
- Medication preparation, mixing, and licensing requirements
- Regulatory and compliance considerations
- Supplier engagement and management:
  - UK-based suppliers
  - European suppliers
  - Reliability, quality, and cost considerations

### Expected Outcomes
- Feasibility report on aseptic capabilities
- Supplier assessment and recommendations
- Compliance gap analysis

## Domain 3: Leadership & Feasibility Support

### Responsibilities
- Strengthening UK operational leadership structures
- Supporting feasibility assessments for new initiatives
- Helping define operating cadence:
  - Huddles
  - Issue tracking
  - Escalation protocols
- Cross-functional and cross-site alignment where relevant

### Expected Outcomes
- Defined leadership structures
- Operating rhythm established
- Clear escalation pathways

## Domain 4: Technology, AI, and Research (Optional / Additive)

### Responsibilities
- Exploring opportunities where technology or AI could support operations
- Input into systems, data, or research-related initiatives
- Leveraging exposure to AI solutions used by other clients

### Expected Outcomes
- Technology opportunity assessment
- Recommendations for system improvements

## Strategic Positioning

Mohammed should be leveraged as a **multiplier**:
- Focus on systems, leadership, and structure
- Not positioned as an executor of routine tasks
- Clear value likely in first 30-60 days if focus is well-defined
- Engagement should remain flexible but intentional to avoid under-utilisation
