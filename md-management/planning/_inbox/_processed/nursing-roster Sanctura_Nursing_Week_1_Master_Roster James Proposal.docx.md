* **Nurse A & B:** 07:00–16:00 (standard weekdays); rotate.
* **Nurse C & D:** Later starts with late cover
* **Monday:** All start early; Nurse C & D start 08:00
* **Anchor nurse:** Rotates weekly
* **Saturday:** Covered by the weekly anchor nurse (08:00–13:00)
* **Following week:** Anchor nurse receives half-day Friday
* **Monday adjustment:** Previous week's anchor nurse works half-day Monday
* **Evenings:** Protected time for protocol study, patient review, documentation, and mandatory end-of-month protocol test
* **Early Mornings:** Preparation treatment rooms general & day's protocols
* **Rotate from Left to Right**
