# Nursing Master Roster — 4-Week Rotating Cycle (James Proposal)

> **Source**: CEO James proposal, routed from planning inbox 2026-03-07
> **Scope**: 4-nurse rotating roster with anchor system; Mon–Sat coverage

---

## Roster Structure

4 nurses (A, B, C, D) rotate on a 4-week cycle. Each week one nurse is designated the **Anchor** nurse, responsible for Saturday morning cover.

**Shift patterns:**
- **Nurse A & B**: 07:00–16:00 standard weekdays; rotate positions
- **Nurse C & D**: Later starts with late cover (11:00–19:00 on most days)
- **Monday**: All start early; anchor nurse starts 08:00
- **Anchor nurse**: Rotates weekly — covers Saturday 08:00–13:00; receives half-day Friday the following week; previous week's anchor works half-day Monday
- **Evenings**: Protected time for protocol study, patient review, documentation, and mandatory end-of-month protocol test
- **Early Mornings**: Preparation of treatment rooms (general and day's protocols)
- **Rotation direction**: Left to right across weeks
