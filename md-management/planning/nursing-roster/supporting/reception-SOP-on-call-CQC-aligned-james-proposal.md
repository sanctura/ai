# SOP: Reception Duty, Morning Clinic Preparation & On-Call Phone Management

> **Source**: CEO James proposal, routed from planning inbox 2026-03-07
> **Alignment**: CQC Fundamental Standards — Safe, Responsive, Effective, Well-Led
> **Status**: Approved | Review: Annual | Owner: Clinic Director

---

## 1. Purpose (CQC: Safe & Responsive)

This SOP ensures that reception services, early morning clinic preparation, and on-call phone management are delivered safely and responsively in line with Care Quality Commission (CQC) Fundamental Standards.

---

## 2. Scope & Responsibility (CQC: Well-Led)

Applies to reception staff, on-call nurses, and duty doctors. All staff must comply with this SOP and escalate concerns appropriately.

---

## 3. Reception Duty — Core Responsibilities (CQC: Responsive & Caring)

Reception staff must:
- Act as the professional first point of contact
- Manage enquiries confidentially and accurately
- Escalate clinical concerns without delay
- Maintain patient dignity and privacy

---

## 4. Morning Clinic Preparation (07:00–08:00) (CQC: Safe & Effective)

Before 08:00, reception must:
- Open clinic and ensure safety
- Switch phones to live answering
- Review urgent messages
- Confirm staff attendance
- Prepare patient list
- Report concerns to clinic lead

---

## 5. Saturday On-Call Phone Management (08:00–12:00) (CQC: Responsive)

Clinic number auto-forwards to primary on-call nurse.

**Failover sequence:**
1. Primary nurse
2. Standby nurse
3. Voicemail

After 12:00, voicemail only.

---

## 6. Call Triage, Escalation & Emergency Referral (CQC: Safe)

- Routine queries managed by nurse.
- Escalate to doctor for red-flag symptoms, deterioration, or adverse events.
- In emergencies, staff must follow the clinic's Emergency and Deteriorating Patient SOP (Emergency Response Policy) held on file and advise emergency services attendance.

---

## 7. Governance & Documentation (CQC: Well-Led)

- All calls documented
- On-call defined and compensated separately
- Confidentiality maintained

---

## 8. Review & Training (CQC: Effective)

Reviewed annually. Staff trained at induction and updates.
