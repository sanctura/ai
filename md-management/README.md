# MD Management System

Your persistent management assistant system for tracking and executing across your dual role as **Managing Director** and **Chief Technology Officer**.

## Purpose

This repository maintains the complete business context, organizational data, strategic alignment, and operational tracking needed to support executive decision-making, planning, and execution.

## Structure

### `/business/`
Core business context, strategy, and organizational structure
- `overview.md` - Company mission, vision, strategy, and targets
- `organization.md` - Org chart, reporting structure, and team composition
- `ceo-directives.md` - CEO feedback, expectations, and strategic guidance

### `/people/`
Team management, performance tracking, and individual development
- `team-roster.md` - Current team members and roles
- `performance/` - Individual performance tracking and notes
- `hiring.md` - Open positions, pipeline, and recruiting status

### `/projects/`
Initiative tracking, priorities, and execution status
- `active.md` - Current projects and their status
- `pipeline.md` - Planned and potential initiatives
- `archive/` - Completed projects

### `/planning/`
Weekly planning, priorities, and decision tracking
- `current-week.md` - This week's priorities and schedule
- `archive/` - Historical weekly plans
- `decisions.md` - Key decisions and their rationale

### `/tracking/`
Risks, opportunities, and ongoing monitoring
- `risks.md` - Current risks and mitigation strategies
- `opportunities.md` - Strategic opportunities and evaluation
- `metrics.md` - Key business metrics and trends

## Usage

1. **Initial Setup**: Fill in the foundational documents in `/business/` and `/people/`
2. **Weekly Rhythm**: Update `/planning/current-week.md` each week
3. **Continuous Updates**: Keep projects, risks, and opportunities current as things change
4. **Review & Reflection**: Use git history to track evolution and learn from patterns

## Interaction Model

Your AI assistant will:
- Read and understand all context before providing guidance
- Help you plan weekly priorities and structure your time
- Track progress on projects and flag risks/blockers
- Provide executive-level recommendations on decisions
- Maintain strategic alignment across initiatives
- Surface patterns and insights from the data
