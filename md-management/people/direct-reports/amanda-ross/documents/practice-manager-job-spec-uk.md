## Practice Manager (UK)

### Role Purpose

Ensure the smooth daily operation of the clinic by executing procedures, coordinating staff activity, and maintaining the environment, patient experience, and operational discipline to the highest standard. Deliver consistent readiness and reliability in every shift.

### Key Responsibilities

* **Daily Clinic Operations**

  * Oversee daily opening/closing routines, room readiness, and on-time clinic starts.
  * Manage day-of scheduling changes, staff coordination, and real-time issue resolution.
  * Lead daily briefings and end-of-day reviews to maintain rhythm and communication.

* **Operational Execution & Checklists**

  * Maintain operational checklists, SOP compliance, and daily readiness standards.
  * Record incidents, complete logs, and ensure immediate escalation where required.
  * Support the adoption and implementation of new operational processes.

* **Facilities & Equipment**

  * Conduct daily facilities and equipment checks; ensure faults are logged and resolved.
  * Manage consumables and inventory within approved limits; maintain stock accuracy.
  * Ensure clinic presentation, cleanliness, and safety standards are upheld.

* **Front-of-House & Patient Experience**

  * Deliver outstanding hospitality and patient service from arrival to departure.
  * Ensure reception readiness, aesthetic standards, and guest comfort.
  * Act as first point of contact for on-site escalations and feedback.

* **Administrative & Financial Processes**

  * Process supplier orders, petty cash, and invoices within delegated limits.
  * Maintain accurate data and records for reporting, training, and compliance.
  * Support payroll and attendance submissions through accurate inputs.

* **Data & Reporting**

  * Ensure operational data accuracy across systems (EMR/CRM, checklists, stock records).
  * Submit daily and weekly metrics to the Founder's Associate for performance tracking.

* **Culture & Team Support**

  * Model professionalism, reliability, and service standards.
  * Support team communication and collaboration across reception, admin, and clinical staff.
  * Participate in briefings, training, and improvement sessions.
