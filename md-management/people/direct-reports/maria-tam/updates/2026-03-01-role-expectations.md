# Maria Tam | Head Nurse (Clinical Lead) — Role Expectations

**Purpose:** To serve as the clinical lead for the nursing team, ensuring patients are treated according to clinical plans and maintaining the pharmacy standards of the London clinic.

## Key Expectations

- **Clinical Leadership:** Act as the lead for the nursing team and ensure all patients are treated according to their specific treatment plans.
- **Prescribing & Medication:** Prescribe medications within your legal qualifications, specifically regarding testosterone, iron infusions, and medication for low neutrophils.
- **Pharmacy Management:** Manage and order all pharmacy stock required for patient treatments.
- **Unit Coordination:** Provide functional management to the administrative and IV nurses to ensure seamless daily clinic operations.
- **Clinical Liaison:** Work in close partnership with Maria Vega to ensure clinical continuity and collaborate with Amanda Ross regarding team coverage.

## The Sanctura Support Structure

To maintain a tight, cohesive unit, the following management framework applies:

### Line Management & Administration

- **Amanda Ross (Practice Manager):** Amanda provides all line management. Both Maria Tam and Maria Vega report to Amanda for all personnel and administrative matters, including leave requests and HR documentation.
- **Leave & Coverage:** If a nurse requires leave, they must go directly to Amanda Ross. Amanda will then liaise with Maria Tam to ensure the clinic has adequate clinical coverage.

### Functional Management

- **Nursing Direction:** Maria Tam provides functional direction to the administrative and IV nurses regarding clinical tasks.
- **Professional Synergy:** Maria Tam and Maria Vega are expected to maintain an open, proactive liaison with each other to ensure the highest standard of patient care and operational efficiency.
