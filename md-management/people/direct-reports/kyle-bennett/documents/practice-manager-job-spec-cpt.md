# Cape Town Practice Manager

### Role Purpose

The Cape Town Practice Manager is responsible for the **smooth, compliant, and high-performing operation of the clinic** — combining daily operational management with strategic oversight.
This role is both **hands-on and leadership-oriented**, ensuring that every patient experience, staff process, and operational system aligns with Sanctura's brand, safety, and service standards.

The role acts as both **leader and operator**: designing and improving systems while also stepping in to deliver and model excellence when needed.

A core expectation of this position is to uphold strict discipline for efficiency and workflow, ensuring all operations align with HR standards and company values.

### Key Responsibilities

#### 1. Operational Leadership

* Oversee the full day-to-day running of the Cape Town clinic, ensuring operational readiness, punctuality, and service excellence.
* Lead morning briefings, coordinate staffing and room allocation, and ensure on-time clinic start and flow throughout the day.
* Monitor and maintain clinic presentation — cleanliness, ambience, signage, and hospitality elements.
* Step in directly to resolve operational gaps or service issues, ensuring continuity of care and brand consistency.
* Maintain the clinic's operational rhythm, aligning with leadership and UK counterparts on standards and reporting.
* Enforce strict discipline and adherence to operational routines to maximise efficiency and workflow consistency.

#### 2. Systems, Processes & Performance

* Own all standard operating procedures (SOPs), ensuring they are current, locally compliant, and effectively implemented.
* Use data dashboards and daily checklists to monitor performance, identifying inefficiencies and driving continuous improvement.
* Maintain a culture of discipline and consistency through daily routines and audits.
* Partner with leadership to design, test, and refine operational systems that improve patient flow, efficiency, and staff performance.

#### 3. Compliance & Governance (South African Context)

* Ensure the clinic meets all applicable **South African regulatory and legislative standards**, including:

  * **POPI Act** (Protection of Personal Information): safeguard patient and staff data in all processes and systems.
  * **Health and Safety Regulations**: maintain safe working conditions, risk registers, and regular inspections.
  * **Fire Safety Requirements**: oversee fire drills, equipment checks, and emergency readiness.
  * **Basic Conditions of Employment Act**: ensure fair scheduling, working hours, and record-keeping compliance.
  * **Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA)**: conduct hazard assessments and ensure staff awareness training.

* Ensure proper documentation, logs, and reporting are maintained for inspections or internal audits.
* Liaise with local authorities or service providers for compliance-related matters when necessary.

#### 4. People & Culture

* Lead the front-of-house, administrative, and support staff — fostering a culture of professionalism, empathy, and accountability.
* Recruit, onboard, and develop team members in collaboration with HR and leadership.
* Conduct performance reviews, coach individuals, and ensure the team is equipped to deliver high standards.
* Promote open communication, teamwork, and a supportive working environment.
* Step in operationally to mentor or fill gaps during staff absences or performance challenges.
* Ensure alignment with HR standards, policies, and disciplinary frameworks to maintain consistency and fairness across all people processes.

#### 5. Financial & Administrative Management

* Manage clinic budgets, operating costs, and petty cash in line with company policy.
* Approve procurement within delegated limits and maintain vendor relationships.
* Monitor supplier performance and ensure timely payments, inventory accuracy, and cost efficiency.
* Support finance with payroll submissions, attendance tracking, and monthly reporting.
* Identify opportunities for operational cost savings without compromising service quality.

#### 6. Patient Experience & Service Delivery

* Ensure every patient receives a seamless, caring, and premium experience from entry to exit.
* Oversee reception, appointment scheduling, and coordination between clinical and administrative teams.
* Respond to feedback and resolve patient concerns promptly and professionally.
* Maintain confidentiality and data integrity in all patient interactions in line with POPI standards.
* Regularly audit the environment and service delivery for compliance with brand and quality standards.

#### 7. Facilities, Equipment & Safety

* Oversee the maintenance and readiness of all clinic spaces, equipment, and consumables.
* Conduct daily readiness and safety checks, reporting faults or hazards immediately.
* Manage relationships with building management, cleaning services, and maintenance providers.
* Ensure all fire safety systems (extinguishers, signage, evacuation plans) are in place and functional.
* Maintain a safe, calm, and aesthetically consistent environment that reflects the brand's quality.

#### 8. Data, Reporting & Continuous Improvement

* Maintain accurate operational data (appointments, incidents, checklists, inventory).
* Generate and analyse weekly performance reports for leadership review.
* Use insights to recommend and implement process improvements.
* Contribute to regional alignment by sharing insights and adopting best practices from the broader Sanctura network.

#### 9. Strategic & Cross-Functional Collaboration

* Partner with leadership to align the Cape Town clinic's performance with group strategy and growth objectives.
* Support rollout of new systems, service lines, or technologies locally.
* Collaborate with marketing, HR, and finance to ensure integrated execution of campaigns and initiatives.
* Represent the clinic in local partnerships, community relations, and external inspections.

### Success Measures

* **Operational Excellence:** Clinic runs smoothly, on time, and without disruption.
* **Compliance:** No regulatory breaches or audit issues under South African law.
* **Financial:** Budget adherence and operational efficiency achieved.
* **Team Engagement:** High morale, low turnover, and strong alignment with values.
* **Patient Experience:** Exceptional feedback and service consistency.
* **Leadership**: Proactive problem-solving, strict operational discipline, and hands-on support demonstrated daily.

### Reporting Structure

* **Reports to:** Managing Director
* **Direct Reports:** Reception, Administrative, and Support Staff
* **Collaborates with:** Medical Officers, Nursing Leads, and Head Office Support Functions (Finance, HR, Marketing, Tech)

### Profile & Attributes

* Minimum 5 years' experience in healthcare, hospitality, or service-based operations management.
* Strong understanding of South African regulatory requirements (POPI, H&S, BCEA, OHSA).
* Hands-on leader — comfortable switching between strategy and execution.
* Excellent people management, organisation, and problem-solving skills.
* Detail-driven with a calm, composed presence and service-first mindset.
* Able to uphold confidentiality, integrity, and brand consistency at all times.
