# **3-MONTH ROADMAP FOR PRACTICE MANAGER (Cape Town)**

---

## **MONTH 1: FOUNDATION & OBSERVATION**

**Theme:** *Understand deeply before you optimise.*

### **Primary Objectives**

* Build relationships and trust with every stakeholder.
* Understand existing systems, workflows, and culture.
* Establish visibility and discipline in daily operations.
* Identify key operational and compliance gaps.

### **Key Focus Areas**

#### **1. Orientation & Relationship Building**

* 1:1 introductions with CEO/Primary Doctor, COO, CTO, Accounting, Head Nurse, Medical Officer, and Patient Care Manager.
* Shadow each team (Reception, Nursing, Admin) for a day to understand pain points.
* Learn the "unwritten rules" - what actually drives daily flow and culture.
* Schedule weekly/bi-weekly touchpoints with CEO & COO.

#### **2. Operational Baseline**

* Review current SOPs, checklists, and performance dashboards.
* Conduct a full clinic audit (facilities, stock, compliance, safety, service flow).
* Observe patient journey from entry to discharge.
* Review roster, punctuality, and workflow discipline.
* Create a "day-in-the-life" map of each staff member to spot inefficiencies.

#### **3. Compliance & Safety Readiness**

* Verify documentation for POPI, OHSA, BCEA compliance.
* Check fire drills, incident logs, H&S registers.
* Identify gaps and create a "Compliance Improvement Log".

#### **4. Quick Wins**

* Reinforce regular morning huddles and end-of-day debriefs.
* Improve one immediate patient-facing area (e.g. ambience, signage, or welcome flow).
* Resolve one recurring frustration for staff (e.g. stock tracking, scheduling clarity).

### **Deliverables by End of Month 1**

* Clinic Audit Report (Operational, Compliance, People)
* Relationship Map (key contacts, responsibilities, dependencies)
* Quick Win Implementation Summary
* Draft 90-day clinic improvement priorities (to share with CEO/COO)

---

## **MONTH 2: SYSTEMISATION & ALIGNMENT**

**Theme:** *Shift from observation to optimisation.*

### **Primary Objectives**

* Begin system and process improvements.
* Establish accountability and reporting rhythms.
* Drive discipline through checklists, SOPs, and dashboards.
* Strengthen your leadership presence and communication rhythm.

### **Key Focus Areas**

#### **1. Operational Systems & SOP Ownership**

* Review, refine, and formalise local SOPs - align with UK HQ where relevant.
* Standardise daily checklists (cleaning, readiness, closing, patient flow).
* Implement weekly data review: patient numbers, delays, satisfaction, incidents.
* Partner with CTO to ensure digital tools and data dashboards are accurate and useful.

#### **2. Team Structure & Accountability**

* Clarify reporting lines and expectations for Reception, Support Staff, and Cleaner.
* Introduce weekly 1:1 check-ins for direct reports.
* Begin coaching conversations for performance consistency.
* Identify training needs - especially in service delivery and compliance.

#### **3. Compliance Strengthening**

* Conduct refresher training for all staff on POPI & H&S.
* Update risk register and fire drill log.
* Ensure all certificates (medical waste, fire, etc.) are valid and filed correctly.

#### **4. Financial & Resource Oversight**

* Review budget allocations, vendor agreements, and petty cash policy.
* Align with Accounting Manager on procurement process and monthly reporting.
* Identify 2-3 potential areas for operational cost savings.

#### **5. Culture & Communication**

* Introduce a monthly "Clinic Wins" update - short meeting or email celebrating improvements, feedback, and excellence.
* Reinforce punctuality, professionalism, and presentation standards.

### **Deliverables by End of Month 2**

* Updated SOPs and Checklists implemented
* Weekly reporting rhythm in place (Ops dashboard)
* POPI & H&S compliance confirmed and documented
* Direct reports have performance goals and check-ins
* Short list of cost-saving or efficiency initiatives

---

## **MONTH 3: PERFORMANCE & CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT**

**Theme:** *Lead decisively, drive results, and set the tone for sustainability.*

### **Primary Objectives**

* Establish measurable performance culture.
* Launch continuous improvement initiatives.
* Strengthen patient experience systems.
* Deliver data-driven updates to leadership.

### **Key Focus Areas**

#### **1. Operational Excellence**

* Track and analyse KPIs: on-time starts, appointment flow, patient satisfaction, incident rates.
* Identify 2-3 process improvements and pilot them (e.g., patient intake process, supply chain efficiency).
* Conduct full "clinic readiness" audit with measurable metrics.

#### **2. Team Development**

* Conduct first quarterly performance reviews for direct reports.
* Reward or recognise high performers publicly.
* Address underperformance early and fairly.
* Collaborate with HR (if centralised) to plan staff development or training.

#### **3. Leadership Reporting & Alignment**

* Present Monthly Operational Report to CEO/COO:
  * Key metrics and trends
  * Wins and issues
  * Upcoming improvement projects
* Engage in monthly leadership roundtable to discuss growth initiatives and cross-functional alignment.

#### **4. Patient Experience Enhancement**

* Review and refine the end-to-end patient journey, ensuring warmth, efficiency, and privacy.
* Introduce a simple feedback loop - post-visit survey or quick digital check-in.
* Analyse feedback trends and close the loop with staff.

#### **5. Strategic Positioning**

* Start planning for the next 6-12 months:
  * Staffing requirements
  * Equipment or technology upgrades
  * Expansion of services or partnerships

### **Deliverables by End of Month 3**

* Performance Report with baseline KPIs
* Completed performance reviews for direct reports
* Patient experience audit and feedback report
* Action plan for next quarter (Q2)
* Recognition and reward mechanism launched

---

## **SUMMARY: SUCCESS ARC**

| Phase | Theme | Leadership Focus | Outcome |
| ----- | ----- | ---------------- | ------- |
| **Month 1** | Foundation | Listen, observe, and stabilise | Trust, clarity, quick wins |
| **Month 2** | Systemisation | Standardise and lead with structure | Efficiency, compliance, discipline |
| **Month 3** | Performance | Optimise, measure, and elevate | Data-driven excellence and team accountability |

## **MANAGERIAL TONE TO ADOPT**

* **Visible & Hands-on:** Walk the floor daily - be the calm presence people anchor to.
* **Structured & Firm:** Clear expectations, consistent follow-up.
* **Empathetic & Service-led:** Model the "Sanctura standard" of care and composure.
* **Data-aware:** Use metrics to guide decisions, not opinions.
