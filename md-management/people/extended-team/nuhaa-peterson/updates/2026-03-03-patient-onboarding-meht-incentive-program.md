# Internal Policy: Patient Onboarding & mEHT Incentive Program

## 1. Program Objective

The objective of this incentive program is to optimize patient conversion rates following clinical consultations and to promote the clinical benefits of **mEHT (Modulated Electro-Hyperthermia)** as part of a comprehensive 6-week treatment protocol.

## 2. Definition of a "Successful Conversion"

A staff member is eligible for the base incentive when a patient:

1. Formally accepts the Doctor's proposed treatment plan and associated costs.
2. Commits to a minimum of **six (6) weeks** of continuous treatment.
3. Completes the booking/scheduling process within **fourteen (14) days** of the initial consultation.

## 3. Incentive Tiers

### Tier 1: Base Referral Fee

* **Amount:** R2,000.00 (Two Thousand Rand) per patient.
* **Eligibility:** Applies to every patient meeting the "Successful Conversion" criteria.
* **Cap:** There is no monthly limit on Tier 1 earnings.

### Tier 2: mEHT Performance Bonus

To reward high-volume onboarding and clinical adherence, an additional bonus is available:

* **Bonus Amount:** R10,000.00 (Ten Thousand Rand) additional lump sum.
* **Criteria:**
  * **Volume:** Five (5) patients converted within any rolling 14-day window.
  * **mEHT Opt-in:** All five (5) patients must include mEHT in their treatment plan.
  * **Clinical Adherence:** All five (5) patients must successfully **complete at least four (4) weeks** of their mEHT protocol.
* **Frequency:** This bonus is limited to **one (1) payout per calendar month.**

---

## 4. Payout Structure & Examples

Incentives are calculated based on the total number of converted patients and their adherence to the mEHT protocol.

| Monthly Conversions | mEHT Opt-in & Completion | Base Pay (R2k/ea) | Bonus Pay | **Total Monthly Incentive** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 5 Patients | 4 of 5 patients | R10,000 | R0 | **R10,000** |
| **5 Patients** | **5 of 5 patients** | **R10,000** | **R10,000** | **R20,000** |
| 8 Patients | 5 of 8 patients* | R16,000 | R10,000 | **R26,000** |
| 10 Patients | 10 of 10 patients | R20,000 | R10,000 | **R30,000** |

**Note: Bonus triggers once the 5-patient mEHT threshold is met within the specified window.*

---

## 5. Terms and Conditions

* **Verification:** Payouts for the Tier 2 Bonus will be processed in the pay cycle following the 4th week of treatment completion for the 5th qualifying patient.
* **Refunds/Cancellations:** If a patient cancels their treatment plan before the 14-day booking window is closed or fails to start treatment, the incentive for that patient will not be realized.
* **Discretionary Review:** The CEO reserves the right to review the quality of conversions to ensure clinical standards are maintained and that patients are not being pressured into plans that do not suit their medical needs.
* **Duration:** This incentive structure is subject to review every 6 months to ensure it aligns with clinic capacity and financial sustainability.
