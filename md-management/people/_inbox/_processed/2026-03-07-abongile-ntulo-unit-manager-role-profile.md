**Role Profile: Unit Manager – Oncology/Chemotherapy Unit**

**Job Title:** Unit Manager  
**Department:** Oncology  
**Reports To:** \[Insert Reporting Line\]  
**Location:** Cape Town  
**Direct Reports:** 3–5  
**Total Team Size: 13**

**Grade Level: D1**

---

**Role Purpose**

To lead and manage the Oncology/Chemotherapy Unit, ensuring the delivery of safe, effective, and compassionate care to patients undergoing chemotherapy. The Unit Manager will oversee clinical operations, staff management, and resource coordination, while maintaining compliance with relevant healthcare standards and protocols.

---

**Qualifications & Experience**

* **Education:** Bachelor’s Degree in Nursing (essential); Postgraduate qualification in Oncology Nursing or Healthcare Management (advantageous)  
* **Registration:** Registered Nurse with relevant professional body  
* **Experience:**   
  * Minimum 7–10 years of nursing experience  
  * At least 3–5 years in a management or leadership role within a clinical setting  
  * Proven experience in oncology and chemotherapy nursing

---

**Key Responsibilities**

* **Clinical Leadership**  
  * Oversee the administration of chemotherapy treatments in accordance with protocols  
  * Ensure adherence to safety standards and infection control procedures  
  * Provide clinical guidance and support to nursing staff  
* **Staff Management**  
  * Lead, mentor, and develop a team of nurses and support staff  
  * Conduct performance reviews and support professional development  
  * Manage staff rostering to ensure adequate coverage and continuity of care  
* **Operational Oversight**  
  * Monitor and maintain chemotherapy equipment and supplies  
  * Ensure accurate documentation and reporting of patient care  
  * Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to optimize patient outcomes  
* **Quality & Compliance**  
  * Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations and internal policies  
  * Participate in audits and implement quality improvement initiatives  
  * Address patient concerns and promote a culture of patient-centered care

---

**Key Competencies**

* Strong clinical knowledge in oncology and chemotherapy  
* Effective leadership and team management skills  
* Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities  
* High attention to detail and organizational skills  
* Ability to work under pressure and manage complex situations
